GO!STEM

What is GO!STEM?

The GO!STEM Summer Program (hosted by the Toronto Chapter of Salesforce Women's Network, and in partnership with hEr VOLUTION) focuses on inpsiring girls, ages 13-17, to explore and study career paths in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering & Math).

In 2018, 31 amazing girls participated in the program to learn first-hand what it's like to work at a tech company like Salesforce and learned how to code their own websites!

What Girls Say About GO!STEM:

Aisha Mohamud | 17

"I never thought that I would pursue a career in STEM, but now thanks to GO!STEM it has influenced me to think about going into Computer Programming. I am thinking of ways to combine American Sign Language (ASL) and tech. Thank you Salesforce and hEr VOLUTION for the amazing experience!"

Daniela Uribe | 14

"I didn't know what coding was in the beginning. After I've been in this program for the past week, I found that coding could be extremely fun and interesting while being to create my own webpage. I recommend everyone to do this program!"

Madhu Manivannan | 15

"During GO!STEM, I got to meet a lot of girls with similar interests to mine, which in turn lets me now know a strong community that enpowers me and other girls to take initiative and action to empower others in return. I think the camp has helped me a lot in finding a mentor and meeting a lot of awesome people in 4 days. Thanks Salesforce and hEr VOLUTION for the great experience!"
